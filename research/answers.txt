Why is it important to ensure access to affordable, reliable, sustainable, and modern energy for all?\nAnswer: Ensuring access to affordable, reliable, sustainable, and modern energy for all is important for several reasons:

1. **Economic Growth**: Access to energy is crucial for economic development and growth. It enables businesses to operate efficiently, creates job opportunities, and drives innovation.

2. **Quality of Life**: Energy is essential for basic needs such as lighting, heating, cooking, and access to clean water. It improves the quality of life for individuals and communities.

3. **Environmental Sustainability**: Sustainable energy sources help reduce greenhouse gas emissions and mitigate climate change. It promotes a cleaner environment and healthier ecosystems.

4. **Social Equity**: Access to energy can reduce inequalities by providing opportunities for education, healthcare, and economic empowerment to all individuals, regardless of their background or location.

5. **Resilience**: Reliable energy sources contribute to the resilience of communities in the face of natural disasters and other challenges. It ensures continuity of essential services and infrastructure.
